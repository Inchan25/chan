Something More Cataclysmic Lies Beyond this Pandemic

Do you feel it too? It sucks, right? Everything sucks! I know positive minds will disagree with this statement but it’s totally okay to feel frustrated. We all feel like we have been pushed away from the realm world and all we wish right now is a mythical hero or a fallen capsule from space that can save us from this pandemic. Sadly, this is reality - it forced us into a new, temporary existence and we all need to suffer from still unanswered questions."When is this gonna end? How long we’ll be here?" And these questions would easily tempt us to step into sadness and fear.

Distress is mostly our villain - a disruption for all of us causing wreaking havoc thoughts and actions that may lead us into something more cataclysmic and would last longer than this pandemic.

28 April

Old Lives Echoing in Isolation.

  • by Chan Adrales
  • Apr 28, 2020
  • Comments

Catching the sunset and relaxation at dusk has always been my favorite pastime, but within this isolation period, I’ve never felt this warm feeling of nostalgia ever before. It feels like the world is slowing down while quickly rewinding the time to bring us back to our old lives. Where there is nothing to do more but to distract ourselves from ourselves inside the mounting pressure of the same four walls by reading books or writing original pieces, watching classic movies or replayed television series, playing or listening to music, drawing or painting imagination that goes beyond the borders and spending the spare time decluttering our life.
